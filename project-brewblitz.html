BrewBlitz Application

BrewBlitz makes it easy to enjoy your perfect brew. Subscribe to coffee grounds or beans, schedule fresh brewed pickups and quickly reorder your favorites. Earn loyalty points and enjoy exclusive deals. With BrewBlitz get the perfect brew delivered to you.

OVERVIEW

I designed this app for a coffee subscription service focused on delivering the perfect brew to your door. I crafted intuitive, user-friendly interfaces and developed high-fidelity prototypes for mobile, allowing users to easily subscribe to coffee grounds or beans, schedule orders for pickup, reorder favorites quickly and take advantage of loyalty points and exclusive deals.

OBJECTIVE

Phone application, visual design, UX design, UI Design, Wireframing, Prototyping

COMPANY

Freelancing

ROLE

UI designer, full time

PERIOD

May 2024 - July 2024

MY DESIGN CONCEPT

Coffee subscription service app

This is the app interface for BrewBlitz, a mobile platform designed to bring the perfect brew to you. The goal was to create a seamless experience where users could easily subscribe to coffee grounds or beans, schedule orders for pickup, reorder favorites and enjoy loyalty points and member deals. I structured the user flow and designed both low-fidelity and high-fidelity prototypes, ensuring a smooth, intuitive interface that makes getting your perfect brew quick and easy.

VISUAL IDENTITY

Logo / Icon / Color Palette / Font Style

The BrewBlitz logo is designed to evoke nostalgia and warmth while maintaining a timeless, simple aesthetic. Its clean, streamlined design represents the speed and efficiency of getting your coffee, while tapping into the comforting feeling of a familiar, favorite brew.

ELEMENTS

Button / List / Status

I designed these UI elements for the BrewBlitz app with a focus on functionality and accessibility. The layout prioritizes quick and easy ordering, featuring clean navigation and clearly defined user actions. I used bold typography and contrasting colors to establish a strong visual hierarchy, while maintaining an overall minimal aesthetic to reduce clutter. This design approach ensures that both frequent and first-time users can navigate the app effortlessly, whether they're ordering coffee for pickup or home brewing.
