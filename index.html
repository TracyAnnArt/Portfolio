About me

Hi, I'm Tracy.

I design with heart and strategy. Blending visual storytelling, UX principles and brand identity to craft meaningful experiences. With a background in fine arts and hands-on experience in branding, UI design and concept development. I work across B2C, nonprofit and startup environments.

My strengths include UI design, character development, color theory, visual branding and crafting visuals that leave a lasting impression. I’m also skilled in prototyping and bringing emotional depth to digital spaces through thoughtful composition and detail.

As a multidisciplinary designer with a focus on visual storytelling and a strong foundation in drawing, painting and user empathy. I blend artistry with strategy to create impactful, user-centered designs. I thrive at the intersection of art and usability.

Tools I love: Figma, Adobe Creative Suite, and a well-worn sketchbook. If you’re crafting something meaningful and need a designer who leads with intention and craft, let’s connect. Reach out via email or LinkedIn.
