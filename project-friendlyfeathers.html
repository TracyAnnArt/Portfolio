FriendlyFeathers Application

Community driven platform that connects bird owners with adoption resources, avian veterinarians, bird-specific information, and fellow enthusiasts.

OVERVIEW

I created this app design for a bird focused social platform. I designed intuitive touchscreen interfaces and developed high-fidelity prototypes for mobile, enabling users to connect with fellow bird owners, find an avian vet, explore adoption opportunities, and access bird specific resources.

OBJECTIVE

Phone application, visual design, UX design, UI Design, Wireframing, Prototyping

COMPANY

Freelancing

ROLE

PERIOD

MY DESIGN CONCEPT

Social platform for bird enthusiasts

This is the app interface for FriendlyFeathers, a mobile platform designed to connect bird owners with adoption opportunities, avian veterinarians, and a community of fellow bird lovers. The goal was to create an intuitive experience where users could easily share updates, access bird-specific resources, and find professional care for their feathered friends. I structured the user flow and designed both low-fidelity and high-fidelity prototypes, ensuring a seamless and visually engaging interface for bird enthusiasts

FLAT ELEMENTS

Button / List / Status

I designed these UI elements for the FriendlyFeathers app with a focus on simplicity and usability. The design incorporates basic shadows for subtle depth, while minimizing other stylistic elements like gradients and textures. This approach ensures the interface remains clean and intuitive, with clear visual hierarchy and easy interaction for users.
