RuinenLust Expeditions Webpage

A curated expedition service offering guided hardhat tours of restricted ruins and forgotten sites. RuinenLust Expeditions handles exclusive access, expert-led experiences and immersive storytelling for history enthusiasts and ruin seekers.

OVERVIEW

I designed this website interface for RuinenLust Expeditions, a niche travel platform offering guided tours of restricted ruins and forgotten historical sites. I created an immersive, user friendly booking experience and developed high fidelity prototypes that showcase available tours, highlight expert guides and reflect the brand's adventurous and scholarly identity. The platform also features sections for the monthly magazine and exclusive events for history enthusiasts.

OBJECTIVE

Website, visual design, UX design, UI Design, Wireframing, Prototyping

COMPANY

Freelancing

ROLE

PERIOD

Feb 2023 - Aug 2023

MY DESIGN CONCEPT

Intuitive booking experience for history and ruin enthusiasts

This is the web interface for RuinenLust Expeditions, a curated travel platform offering exclusive hardhat tours of restricted ruins and forgotten historical sites. The goal was to create an intuitive, visually compelling experience that captures the spirit of urban exploration while making it easy for users to browse tours, reserve slots and learn about each location's historical significance. I developed both low-fidelity and high-fidelity prototypes, focusing on a seamless user flow that reflects the brand's adventurous, scholarly and exclusive identity.

ELEMENTS

Button / List / Status indicator

I designed these UI elements for the RuinenLust Expeditions platform to balance sophistication with a sense of discovery. Subtle shadows create gentle depth, guiding users through the interface without visual clutter. By minimizing textures and gradients, the design remains clean and elegant. Allowing the spirit of adventure and historical intrigue to shine through with clarity and ease of interaction.
